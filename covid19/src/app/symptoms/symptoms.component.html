When anyone is affected by this virus, it doesn't make you sudden sick. But if your immune system is healthy, then our efforts is to get rid of this. If your immunity is high, then there are fewer chances to get affected by the virus. For example, our immune system raises our body temperature to make it hotter to kill these viruses.

Everyone's body is different, so the symptoms are caused by our immune system, not by the virus itself. Different people have different symptoms when they are affected by the same infection or illness.

Novel coronavirus (Covid-19) is an illness of respiratory, and it affects the parts of our body that helps us to breathe: mainly our throat, nose, and lungs. In more severe cases, the infection cause pneumonia, severe acute respiratory syndrome (SARS), kidney failure, and death. If you are affected with novel coronavirus, the symptoms may include:

  • Coughing/sneezing
  • High Fever
  • Sore throat
  • Fatigue
  • Shortness of breath/ difficulty in breathing
  • Muscle pain/ tiredness

    • In severe cases :-
  • Pneumonia
  • Severe acute respiratory syndrome
  • Kidney failure

    • How does the novel coronavirus (COVID-19) spread, and how can we observe it?

    Novel coronavirus (COVID-19) is spreading between people to people when a sick person coughs or sneezes in front of others. The coronavirus can impact us if any affected person sneezes or coughs on other people or things. The virus can also harm us if the infected person have coughed or sneezed on a surface (like a door handle, balcony grill), and we touch the particular surface or the person's hand. The droplets of the infected person infects our hands and the viruses is transferred through our nose, eyes, or mouth into our body.
